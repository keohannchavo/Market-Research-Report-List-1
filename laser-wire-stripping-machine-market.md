<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Stripping Machine</li><li>YAG Laser Stripping Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Wire Stripping Machine market primarily features two types: CO2 Laser Stripping Machines and YAG Laser Stripping Machines. CO2 Laser Stripping Machines utilize carbon dioxide lasers, ideal for stripping thicker insulations and achieving high-speed operations. Conversely, YAG Laser Stripping Machines use neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet lasers, known for precision and effectiveness on delicate materials and thinner wires. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, offering accuracy and efficiency in wire processing and ensuring minimal damage to underlying conductors.</p></p>
