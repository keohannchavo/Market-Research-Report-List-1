<p><p>The polyethylene valves market comprises several types, primarily including ball valves, butterfly valves, and various other valves. Ball valves feature a spherical disc that controls flow, providing a tight seal and quick operation, making them ideal for on/off applications. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to regulate flow, offering lightweight design and space efficiency, suitable for large volume control. Other types encompass valves like gate, check, and diaphragm valves, each serving specialized functions in fluid management across various industries.</p></p>
