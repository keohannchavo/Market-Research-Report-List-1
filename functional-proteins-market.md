<p><p>The functional proteins market encompasses several types, including hydrolysates, whey protein concentrates, and whey protein isolates. Hydrolysates are pre-digested proteins that offer quick absorption, making them ideal for recovery. Whey protein concentrates contain 70-80% protein and retain beneficial nutrients, suitable for general supplementation. In contrast, whey protein isolates boast a higher protein content (90% or more) and minimal fat and lactose, catering to athletes seeking high-purity options. Together, these products serve diverse nutritional needs in sports and functional food sectors.</p></p>
