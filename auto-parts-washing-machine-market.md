<p><p>The auto parts washing machine market includes three main types: ultrasound, spray, and laser cleaning systems. Ultrasound machines use high-frequency sound waves to create cavitation bubbles that effectively dislodge contaminants from surfaces. Spray washing systems rely on high-pressure jets of cleaning solution to remove dirt and grease, making them suitable for larger parts. Laser cleaning technology employs focused laser beams to vaporize contaminants, offering precise and eco-friendly cleaning without damaging the underlying materials. Each type caters to different cleaning needs and applications.</p></p>
