<p><strong>The Porous Silicon Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microporous Silicon Substrates</li><li>Mesoporous Silicon Substrates</li><li>Macroporous Silicon Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The porous silicon substrates market is categorized into three main types based on pore size. Microporous silicon substrates feature pore sizes less than 2 nm, suitable for applications in gas sensors and catalysis. Mesoporous silicon substrates have pore sizes ranging from 2 to 50 nm, making them ideal for drug delivery and photovoltaic applications. Macroporous silicon substrates, with pore sizes exceeding 50 nm, are used in applications such as tissue engineering and biocompatible materials, leveraging their structural properties.</p></p>
