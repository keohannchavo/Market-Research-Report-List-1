<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Infrared Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vanadium Oxide (VOx)</li><li>Amorphous Silicon (A-Si)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared imager market comprises primarily two types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors utilize vanadium oxide as the sensing material, offering high sensitivity and operational efficiency, making them suitable for various applications like security and military. In contrast, A-Si detectors employ amorphous silicon, known for lower production costs and flexibility, which makes them ideal for consumer electronics and automotive uses. Both technologies provide effective thermal imaging solutions across diverse sectors.</p></p>
