The dynamic type employs oscillatory testing to assess viscosity and elasticity in real-time, making it ideal for polymers and complex fluids. Capillary types utilize a narrow channel to analyze material flow under pressure, typically suited for lower-viscosity fluids. Other systems may include specialized setups for unique applications, addressing specific material behaviors beyond conventional methods.</p></p>
