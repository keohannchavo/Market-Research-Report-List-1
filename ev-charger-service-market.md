<p><p>The EV charger service market consists of three primary types: AC charging stations, DC charging stations, and wireless charging stations. AC charging stations utilize alternating current and are typically slower, suited for home or workplace use. DC charging stations provide direct current, enabling rapid charging ideal for public locations and long-distance travel. Wireless charging stations offer a convenient, contactless charging method, incorporating electromagnetic induction technology. Each type addresses specific consumer needs and charging scenarios, contributing to the overall growth of electric mobility.</p></p>
