<p><p>Connected Vehicles Technology encompasses various communication types enhancing road safety and efficiency. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) enables cars to share information about speed, location, and direction, preventing collisions. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) facilitates communication between vehicles and traffic systems, optimizing traffic flow and signal timing. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) technology helps vehicles interact with pedestrians, enhancing safety by alerting drivers to nearby individuals. Together, these technologies create a networked ecosystem that improves transportation safety and efficiency.</p></p>
