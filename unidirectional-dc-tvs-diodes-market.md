<p><p>The Unidirectional DC TVS Diode market is categorized into two primary types: High Impedance and Low Impedance. High Impedance TVS diodes are designed to offer minimal leakage current and are typically used in applications where power efficiency is critical, providing protection without significantly affecting circuit performance. In contrast, Low Impedance TVS diodes have lower clamping voltages and faster response times, making them suitable for high-speed applications with heavier surge conditions, ensuring effective transient voltage protection.</p></p>
