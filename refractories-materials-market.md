<p><ul><li>Acidic</li><li>Neutral</li><li>Basic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractory materials are classified into three main types based on their chemical behavior in relation to slag and other molten materials: acidic, neutral, and basic. Acidic refractories, like silica, are resistant to acidic environments but react with basic materials. Neutral refractories, such as alumina, are stable in both acidic and basic conditions. Basic refractories, including magnesite, are resistant to basic slags and are prone to reaction with acidic materials. Each type serves specific applications in industries like metal production and glass manufacturing.</p></p>
