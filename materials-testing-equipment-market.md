<p><p>The materials testing equipment market encompasses a variety of devices used to evaluate the mechanical properties of materials. Universal testing machines assess tensile strength and compression, while servohydraulic testing machines provide controlled loading for dynamic testing. Hardness testing equipment measures material resistance to deformation. Impact testing equipment evaluates toughness by measuring energy absorption upon impact. Tensile testing focuses on elongation and strength under tension. Together, these tools are essential for quality assurance in manufacturing and materials research.</p></p>
