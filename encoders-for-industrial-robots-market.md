<p><strong>The Encoders for Industrial Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Relative Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robot encoders market is primarily divided into two types: absolute encoders and relative encoders. Absolute encoders provide unique position data for each shaft angle, making them ideal for precise applications, as they maintain position information even during power loss. In contrast, relative encoders measure the movement from a defined starting point, offering simpler designs and lower costs. Both encoder types are essential for enhancing robotic precision, efficiency, and functionality in various industrial applications.</p></p>
