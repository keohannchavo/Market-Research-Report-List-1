<p><ul><li>Fully Customized</li><li>Semi-Customized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable ASIC market is divided into two main types: fully customized and semi-customized. Fully customized ASICs are designed from the ground up for specific applications, offering optimal performance and efficiency but requiring significant design time and cost. In contrast, semi-custom ASICs utilize pre-designed components and logic cells that can be reconfigured, providing a balance between customization and cost-effectiveness. This allows for quicker deployment and adaptation to various applications, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
