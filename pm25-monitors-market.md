<p><strong>The PM2.5 Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PM2.5 monitors market primarily includes two types: TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitors and Beta Attenuation monitors. TEOM monitors measure particulate matter by analyzing mass changes in a vibrating tapered element, providing real-time data and high sensitivity. In contrast, Beta Attenuation monitors assess PM2.5 concentrations by measuring the decrease in beta radiation through collected particles, offering accurate long-term measurements. Both technologies serve critical roles in air quality monitoring and environmental assessment, catering to regulatory and research needs.</p></p>
