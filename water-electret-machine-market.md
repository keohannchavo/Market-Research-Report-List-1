<p><ul><li>Dry Method</li><li>Wet Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Electret Machine market is divided into two main types: Dry Method and Wet Method. The Dry Method utilizes electrostatic principles to create electric charges in water without the need for additional liquids or solvents, making it energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. Conversely, the Wet Method involves using water and often chemicals to induce electret properties, offering enhanced performance in applications requiring higher charge retention. Both methods serve distinct industrial needs, catering to various sectors such as agriculture and water treatment.</p></p>
