<p><ul><li>Optical Displacement Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic Displacement Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handset proximity sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: optical displacement sensors and ultrasonic displacement sensors. Optical displacement sensors use light to detect the presence of nearby objects, allowing devices to turn off screens during calls to prevent accidental touches. In contrast, ultrasonic displacement sensors utilize sound waves to measure distance, effectively detecting obstructions and adjusting device functions accordingly. Both types enhance user experience by ensuring seamless interactions and conserving battery life in mobile devices.</p></p>
