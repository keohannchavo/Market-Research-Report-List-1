<p><p>Railway buffer stops are crucial safety devices at the end of train tracks, designed to prevent accidents. </p><p>Frictional buffer stops use material to absorb energy during impacts. Fixed buffer stops are rigid structures offering strong stopping power. Hydraulic/mechanical buffer stops utilize hydraulic force or mechanical systems for impact absorption. Wheel stops function by allowing train wheels to roll into them, reducing impact. Folding buffer stops collapse under pressure to minimize damage, while specially designed buffer stops cater to unique railway needs, enhancing overall safety and performance.</p></p>
