<p><strong>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography</li><li>Tympanic Membrane Displacement (TMD)</li><li>Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter</li><li>MRI/CT</li><li>Fundoscopy (Papilledema)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring Device market includes various techniques for assessing intracranial pressure without invasive methods. Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography uses ultrasound to evaluate blood flow changes in the brain, while Tympanic Membrane Displacement measures pressure variations through the tympanic membrane. The Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter (ONSD) assesses swelling of the optic nerve as an ICP indicator. MRI/CT imaging provides detailed brain structure information, and fundoscopy examines the retina for signs of elevated pressure via papilledema.</p></p>
