<p><p>The Self-Compacting Concrete (SCC) market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Powder Type SCC** utilizes a high volume of powder materials, promoting fluidity and stability through a well-graded aggregate mix. </p><p>2. **Viscosity Agent Type SCC** incorporates additives that enhance its viscosity, ensuring better control and reduced risk of segregation during placement. </p><p>3. **Combination Type SCC** merges both powder and viscosity agents, achieving optimal workability and structural integrity, making it suitable for complex forms and congested reinforcements.</p></p>
