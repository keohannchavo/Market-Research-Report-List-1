<p><p>The brass valves market is segmented into several types, including gate valves, globe valves, ball valves, check valves, and others. Gate valves are used for on/off flow control, providing minimal resistance when fully open. Globe valves allow precise flow regulation. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities and durability. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring fluid moves in one direction. Other types may include pressure relief valves and control valves, catering to diverse industrial applications, enhancing overall system efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
