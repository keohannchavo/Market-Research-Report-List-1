<p><p>The Indirect Time-of-Flight (ToF) Sensors market is primarily categorized into Optical ToF Sensors and Electromagnetic ToF Sensors. Optical ToF Sensors utilize light pulses to measure distances by calculating the time it takes for reflected light to return, making them ideal for applications like 3D imaging and gesture recognition. Conversely, Electromagnetic ToF Sensors employ electromagnetic waves, such as radio frequencies, to gauge distances, finding use in various industries, including automotive and robotics for obstacle detection and navigation.</p></p>
