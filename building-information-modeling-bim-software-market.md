<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) software encompasses various market types, each enhancing project management. The 3D BIM focuses on design modeling, creating detailed visual representations of structures. 4D BIM incorporates time, simulating construction dynamics to optimize scheduling. 5D BIM adds cost management, integrating financial data for accurate budgeting. 6D BIM addresses built facilities management, ensuring efficient operation post-construction. Finally, 7D BIM emphasizes environmental protection, assessing sustainability and reducing the ecological impact throughout the building lifecycle.</p></p>
