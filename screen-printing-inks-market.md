<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen printing inks market primarily consists of solvent-based and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents to dissolve pigments, offering vibrant colors and excellent adhesion on various substrates, making them ideal for industrial applications. Conversely, water-based inks use water as a solvent, providing a more eco-friendly option with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They are increasingly popular for textiles and packaging due to their ease of cleanup and reduced environmental impact, catering to the growing demand for sustainable products.</p></p>
