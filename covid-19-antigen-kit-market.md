<p><p>The COVID-19 Antigen Kit market comprises primarily two types: Rapid Detection Kits and PCR Kits. Rapid Detection Kits provide quick results by identifying specific antigens related to the virus, making them suitable for point-of-care testing. In contrast, PCR Kits utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to detect viral RNA, offering higher sensitivity and accuracy, though results take longer. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing COVID-19, aiding in timely responses and effective management of the pandemic.</p></p>
