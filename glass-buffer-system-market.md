<p><ul><li>Vertical Film Management System</li><li>Horizontal Film Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass buffer system market is categorized into two main types: vertical film management systems and horizontal film management systems. Vertical film management systems are designed to accommodate glass sheets in an upright position, optimizing space and facilitating easy access during handling. In contrast, horizontal film management systems arrange glass sheets flat, often providing enhanced stability and ease of stacking. Both systems are essential for efficient glass processing and storage, catering to different operational needs in the glass industry.</p></p>
