<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Black Box market can be categorized into two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated black boxes are built directly into the vehicle's systems, offering seamless data collection and real-time monitoring of vehicle performance and driving behavior. In contrast, portable black boxes are standalone devices that can be easily installed or removed, providing flexibility for users to track and analyze data across different vehicles. Both types enhance safety, support accident investigations, and improve overall vehicle efficiency.</p></p>
