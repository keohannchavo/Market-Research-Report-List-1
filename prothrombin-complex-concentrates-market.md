<p><p>Prothrombin Complex Concentrates (PCCs) are used primarily for managing patients with coagulopathy, especially those on anticoagulant therapy. The market is divided into two main types: 3F and 4F PCCs. 3F Prothrombin Complex Concentrates contain three clotting factors (II, IX, and X) and are typically used for specific indications. In contrast, 4F Prothrombin Complex Concentrates include an additional factor (VII), offering a broader application for reversing anticoagulation and improving hemostasis in various clinical scenarios.</p></p>
