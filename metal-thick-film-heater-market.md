<p><p>The Metal Thick Film Heater market comprises two primary types: flat heaters and tubular heaters. Tubular heaters, on the other hand, are versatile and can be used in various configurations for heating liquids or gases in industrial settings. Both types utilize a thick film resistive layer for efficient heat generation, catering to diverse applications across multiple sectors.</p></p>
